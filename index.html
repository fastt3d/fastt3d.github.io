Method

In this work, we introduce Fast-T3D, a pioneering framework for text-to-3D generation, illustrated in following Figure. The overall architecture of Fast-T3D consists of two main components: (1) 3D Prior-Binding Module to construct text-3D association with scarce 3D priors. (2) 2D Multi-Gradient Optimizer to inherit generation capabilities and facilitate efficient inference from comprehensive training scope.
