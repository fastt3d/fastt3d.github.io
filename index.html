Abstract

With the rise in demand for 3D asset creation, text-to-3D generation technologies have emerged as a pivotal solution. Existing methods generally adopt either learning-based strategies, utilizing text-3D data, or optimization-based strategies with the 2D gradient from 2D generation models. However, both strategies exhibit imbalances in terms of diversity, efficiency, and quality, significantly limiting their practicality.

To take a step toward practical deployment, we introduce an innovative framework for Fast Text-to-3D generation (Fast-T3D) that also ensures generation quality and diversity. Harnessing the strengths of both learning-based and optimization-based methods, our framework has two technical contributions. Firstly, our proposed 3D Prior-Binding Module effectively learns text-3D association from scarce 3D priors, eliminating the need for extensive datasets and boosting geometric consistency. Secondly, our 2D Multi-Gradient Optimizer expands training across diverse text prompts, inheriting generation scalability and facilitating fast-forward inference.

Compared to existing state-of-the-art methods, Fast-T3D boosts a remarkable 3000X speed enhancement over ProlificDreamer, while also outperforming in qualitative and quantitative evaluations, demonstrating the superiority in the textual and geometric consistency of 3D generations.

Method

In this work, we introduce Fast-T3D, a pioneering framework for text-to-3D generation, illustrated in following Figure. The overall architecture of Fast-T3D consists of two main components: (1) 3D Prior-Binding Module to construct text-3D association with scarce 3D priors. (2) 2D Multi-Gradient Optimizer to inherit generation capabilities and facilitate efficient inference from comprehensive training scope.
